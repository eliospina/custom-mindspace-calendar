Privacy

Mindspace Calendar is an open-source UX demo. It is not a Google product.

What we access: Google Calendar events (read-only) when you tap Sign in.

What we store: Your OAuth token in this browser’s session storage only — enough to keep you signed in until you close the tab or sign out. We do not send your calendar data to our servers. There is no backend database.

What we do not do: Create, edit, or delete events. Sell data. Share data with third parties.
