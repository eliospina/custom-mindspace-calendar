Privacy

Mindspace Calendar is an open-source UX demo. It is not a Google product.

What we access: Google Calendar events (read-only) only if you self-host with Sign in enabled.

What we store: Nothing on a server. The public demo collects no data. Self-hosted sign-in may keep an OAuth token in your browser session only.

What we do not do: Create, edit, or delete events. Sell data. Share data with third parties.
